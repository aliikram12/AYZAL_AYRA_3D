Our Legacy

Crafting Invisible Armor

AYZAL AYRA was founded on a simple philosophy: scent should not just be worn; it should define an atmosphere. By harvesting the world's most elusive raw ingredients, we create olfactory structures that leave an eternal trace.

2021 • The Genesis

A private blend laboratory is established in search of the perfect balance between rare oud resins and floral absolutes.

2023 • Royal Endorsement
